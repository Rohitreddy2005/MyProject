The Smart Traffic Management System (STMS) is a prototype developed to showcase how AI can optimize city-wide traffic in real-time. It combines map-based monitoring, signal automation, emergency handling, congestion detection, and route diversion.

üîë Key Features

  • Map-based monitoring: All traffic signals displayed on a live city map.
  • Signal automation: AI dynamically adjusts signal timings based on congestion & flow.
  • Emergency preemption: Ambulances & VIP convoys are detected, creating a green corridor.
  • Road events: Accidents, construction, or closures update map in real-time & suggest diversions.
  • Analytics: Graphs, pie charts, and CSV export for traffic history at each signal.
